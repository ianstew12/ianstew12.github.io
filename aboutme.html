About Me

A glimpse into my what my life involved before it was consumed by coding bootcamp


Devcodecamp owns the next three months of my life. I've accepted this. Prior to committing myself full-time to coding, however, I did have a life. I consider myself fortunate to have multiple hobbies I am passionate about. Most of my hobbies are active; I enjoy running, cycling, hiking, camping, and just about any other excuse to get outside and move around. When I get to do these things in a new city or country, I am especially happy. Recently, I added astronomy to my list of interests (hence the animation below.)


The code for the following animation of the solar system was written by Wayne Dunkley (@wayne_dunkley) and taken from http://codepen.io/waynedunkley/pen/YPJWaz/. I used this animation to learn how to insert an animation, not to portray that I am (yet) capable of writing such code myself.
